--- layout: page title: Research Competition permalink: "/research/" image: assets/images/empty_sp.png --- # About the Research Paper Competition The **KAFTA Research Paper Competition** is a premier initiative dedicated to fostering innovation and professional excellence among the next generation of food scientists. [cite_start]By recognizing the outstanding research of Korean and Korean-American graduate students and post-doctoral associates, we aim to bridge the gap between academic discovery and industrial application[cite: 6]. ### **Purpose and Vision** [cite_start]The primary objective of this competition is to empower young professionals in the United States and Korea to develop transformative food technologies[cite: 6]. [cite_start]We provide a prestigious platform for emerging scholars to showcase research that demonstrates exceptional technical quality, originality, and the potential to advance the global food industry. ### **Award Pillars** Our competition honors excellence across two distinct categories: * [cite_start]**The Joh Foundation Research Award (Category 1):** Focuses on advancements in food chemistry and processing, particularly those related to functional ingredients for the food industry[cite: 6]. * [cite_start]**The Swagger Foods Award (Category 2):** Recognizes impactful contributions across the broader spectrum of food science and technology[cite: 6]. ### **Commitment to Excellence** [cite_start]To ensure the highest scholarly standards, all submissions undergo a rigorous **blind review process** conducted by a committee of six distinguished experts from both the United States and Korea. [cite_start]Submissions are evaluated on technical merit, clarity of presentation, and their overall contribution to the field[cite: 25]. [cite_start]Furthermore, all awardees commit to the long-term dissemination of knowledge by publishing their recognized research in **SCI or SCIE journals**[cite: 6]. --- ### **Past Winners** *Below is a record of the distinguished researchers who have been recognized for their excellence in previous years.* | Year | Category | Award | Name | Affiliation | | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | | 2024 | Joh Foundation | 1st Place | [Name] | [University/Institution] | | 2024 | Swagger Foods | Winner | [Name] | [University/Institution] | | 2023 | Joh Foundation | 1st Place | [Name] | [University/Institution] | | 2023 | Swagger Foods | Winner | [Name] | [University/Institution] | [cite_start]*Note: For the 2025 competition, the 1st place awardee in Category 1 is invited to present their findings during the KAFTA In-Person Meeting[cite: 6, 7].*
